Free Transport Options

No Splitting Up

The following outlines all the transport options avalible for participants. There is a series of free bus and tram routes which can be utilised if needed. It is adament that a team must remain together at all times - no splitting up. Below is the maps and timetables for the free routes.

Free Bus Routes

99A, 99c, 98A, 98C

Adelaide Tram Routes

All trams North of the South Terrace Station are Free

Other Transportation Methods

  • No personal cars, motorbikes, etc. unless they happen to be a Relient Robin as seen in Only Fools and Horses.
  • No taxis, ubers, etc. unless they happen to be a London style Black Cab.
  • Personal bikes, scooters, skate-boards, etc. are allowed, provided that a helmet is worn at all times.
  • Public bikes and scooters are allowed, provided that all rules around these are followed.
  • Wheelies, roller skates, roller blades, etc. are allowed, provided that a helmet is worn at all times.
  • No teleportation or time travel, including (although not limited too) any TARDIS, DeLorian, Time-Vortex Manipulator, etc.
  • Other public transport options are available and allowed, although none are free of charge.
  • Sky or sea vessles are not allowed.
  • Living transport is not allowed, including (although not limited too) horses, donkies, humans, muels, cows, etc.
