Team Requirements

Entry Fee: $30 (external site) | Persons Count: 2 - 7

The team structure has a few set requirements to help ensure the day runs as smooth as possible. Teams will have a minimum of 2 people and a maximum of 7.

There are three roles that are required for each team to have. Each of these must be a different person.

  • Captain: Leads their team to victory. Performs tasks including team registration and similar admin jobs. Is the only one to submit answers to Tamsin.
  • Rules Manager: Ensures that they know the rules and that their team follows them.
  • Team Member: All other team members. They help solve puzzles, identify routes to locations, and are just as important as all others in the team.
    (Not applicable for teams of 2).
There is also an entry fee of $30 per team to cover costs of the day including printing (2D and 3D), laser cutting, food, etc. The link to the fee submission is above.
