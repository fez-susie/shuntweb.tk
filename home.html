About Scavenger Hunt

The Scavenger Hunt is a fun, friendly competition where participants work in teams to solve puzzles that lead to locations.

Get a team together and nominate a team captain to register your team of the registration page.

If you don't have a team, that's okay - fill out the individual registration and we will place you in one.

The Map

The map below outlines the area in which the activity will take place.

  • The purple house icon at Victoria Square is the rendezvous point. All major events across the day happen here. If at anytime a team is split up or something goes wrong, all team members must report here.
  • The yellow starts represent the four squares and Rundle Mall. Use these as a safe place to rest and stay whilst puzzle solving. These roughly represent the bounding areas of the day.
  • The blue polygon represents the boundaries. All puzzles are within this zone. You may leave the area but there is no need to do as such.
